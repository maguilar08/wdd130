Have An Adventure

Make Memories with Dry Oar

Our purpose at Dry Oar is to offer exhilarating white water rafting experiences that push boundaries, foster a sense of adventure, and create lasting connections with the great outdoors. Whether you're a seasoned rafter seeking the ultimate adrenaline rush or a newcomer looking to embark on your first river adventure, we're here to make your dreams of conquering the rapids a reality.

View Trips!

Thrilling Rafting Expeditions

Join us for heart-pounding rides down some of the most iconic rivers in the world. From the rugged canyons of the Colorado River to the pristine waters of the Futaleufú in Patagonia, our expeditions cater to all skill levels and interests.

Expert Guides

Our team of experienced and certified guides are passionate about sharing their love for the river and ensuring your safety at every turn. They'll navigate you through challenging rapids, point out hidden gems along the way, and regale you with fascinating stories about the history and ecology of the area.

Customized Adventures

Whether you're planning a family vacation, a corporate retreat, or a solo adventure, we can tailor our rafting trips to suit your preferences. Choose from half-day excursions to multi-day expeditions, camping under the stars or staying in cozy lodges – the options are endless!

Scenic Beauty

Immerse yourself in the stunning landscapes that surround our rafting sites. From towering cliffs and lush forests to cascading waterfalls and abundant wildlife, each journey with Dry Oar is a feast for the senses.

Memorable Experiences

At Dry Oar, we believe that the best adventures are the ones you remember long after the journey ends. Whether it's conquering your first Class III rapid, bonding with friends around the campfire, or simply marveling at the beauty of nature, we're committed to creating moments that will stay with you for a lifetime.
